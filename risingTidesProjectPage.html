Rising Tides

In "Rising Tides," you must outpace the relentless rise of water in a captivating 2D platformer. Navigate through the challenging level as the water level climbs, forcing you to think quickly and move faster. You aim to ascend to higher ground to survive while collecting as many coins as possible to boost your score.
